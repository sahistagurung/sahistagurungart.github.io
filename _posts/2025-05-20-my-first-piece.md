## Golden Peaks

This piece was inspired by the majesty of the high mountains at sunrise. I wanted to focus on the contrast between the cold, jagged shadows of the base and the warm, almost ethereal gold that touches the summits.

The addition of the small yellow tents and the prayer flags provides a sense of scale—reminding us of how small we are in the face of such ancient giants, yet how we still seek to reach them.
