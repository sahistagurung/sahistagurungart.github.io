## Starry Night Reimagined

In this piece, I wanted to capture the overwhelming rhythm of existence. The canvas is dominated by concentric spirals in hues of yellow and deep blue, representing the various "cycles" we inhabit—time, history, and the self.

The swirling energy of the sky contrasts with the grounded, dark silhouette in the foreground. It’s a reflection on the human condition: we are all part of the same grand pattern, yet we experience our journeys in profound, singular solitude. The thick, visible brushstrokes add a textured, physical feel to a cosmic concept.
