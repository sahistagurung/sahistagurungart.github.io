## Circle of Life

In this piece, I wanted to capture the overwhelming rhythm of existence. The canvas is dominated by concentric spirals in hues of ochre, crimson, and rust, representing the various "cycles" we inhabit—family, time, history, and the self.

The three silhouetted figures are placed intentionally. Though they share the same canvas and are surrounded by the same swirling energy, they remain in their own distinct "bubbles." It’s a reflection on the human condition: we are all part of the same grand pattern, yet we experience our journeys in profound, singular solitude. The rough texture of the paint adds a grounded, earthy feel to an otherwise cosmic concept.

---

### About the Piece

- Medium: Acrylic on Canvas
- Inspiration: The interplay between individual consciousness and the  "universal hum" of life.
- Year: 2025
