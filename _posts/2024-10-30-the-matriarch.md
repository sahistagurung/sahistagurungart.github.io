## The Matriarch

In this study, I wanted to focus entirely on texture. The elephant's skin is like a map of its life—every wrinkle and fold tells a story of survival and time.

By keeping the background pure black, the focus remains entirely on the form and the light hitting the tusks. This piece was a challenge in patience, capturing the small details that make these creatures so majestic.
