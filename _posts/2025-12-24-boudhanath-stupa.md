## Boudhanath Stupa

This painting is a tribute to one of the most sacred sites in Kathmandu. I focused on the striking 'Wisdom Eyes' of the Buddha, which symbolize all-seeing compassion. 

The background is filled with warm, golden tones to mimic the glow of a sunset, while the prayer flags add a rhythmic splash of primary colors. I wanted the viewer to feel the stillness and the spiritual energy that surrounds this monument.

### About the Piece
