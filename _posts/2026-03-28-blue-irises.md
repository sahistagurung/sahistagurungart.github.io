## Azure Irises

Inspired by the expressive nature of irises, this piece focuses on the harmony between complementary colors. By placing the cool, deep blues of the petals against a warm yellow field, I wanted to create a sense of energy that feels both classic and contemporary.

The thick application of paint on the petals adds a tactile quality, emphasizing the organic, flowing shapes of the flowers as they spill from the vase.
