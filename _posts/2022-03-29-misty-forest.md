## Misty Forest

With this piece, I wanted to explore the concept of 'negative space' using fog. By layering the trees with varying levels of transparency, I tried to create a sense of infinite depth.

It’s meant to evoke a feeling of early morning stillness—that moment before the world fully wakes up, where everything feels muffled and calm.

---

### About the Piece

- **Medium:** Acrylic on Canvas
- **Style:** Atmospheric Minimalism
