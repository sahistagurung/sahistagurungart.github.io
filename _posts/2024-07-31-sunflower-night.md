## Sunflower Night

This piece is a dialogue between two of my favorite inspirations. I wanted to see how the vibrant, sun-soaked energy of a sunflower field would react to the deep, swirling blues of a starry night. 

The sunflowers are painted with thick, energetic strokes to give them a sense of life even in the dark. It represents hope and brightness remaining steadfast even when surrounded by the vast unknown.

---

### About the Piece

- **Medium:** Acrylic on Canvas
- **Inspiration:** Vincent van Gogh's Sunflowers and Starry Night
- **Year:** 2024
