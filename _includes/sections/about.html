About Me

I am an Artist and Computer Science student currently transitioning into undergraduate studies. My work exists at the intersection of traditional Himalayan aesthetics and modern digital exploration. Based in Kathmandu, Nepal, I draw inspiration from the spiritual architecture and natural majesty of my surroundings to create emotive, textured pieces.

My portfolio, Sahista Gurung Art, represents a journey through various mediums—from detailed graphite studies of wildlife to vibrant, large-scale acrylic landscapes. Beyond the canvas, I am deeply invested in AI and Web Development, viewing code as just another medium for creative expression and problem-solving.

As I move into my undergraduate career, my goal is to continue bridging the gap between the fine arts and technical innovation. I am looking for opportunities to collaborate on projects that require a sharp eye for design and a logical approach to development.
