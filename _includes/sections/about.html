About Me

I am an Artist whose work exists at the intersection of traditional Himalayan aesthetics and modern digital exploration. Based in Kathmandu, Nepal, I draw inspiration from the spiritual architecture and natural majesty of my surroundings to create emotive, textured pieces.

My portfolio, Sahista Gurung Art, represents a journey through various mediums—from detailed graphite studies of wildlife to vibrant, large-scale acrylic landscapes. Each work is a reflection of my cultural heritage and an exploration of the emotive power of the natural world.

My goal is to continue exploring the connection between fine arts and life, blending creativity with innovation. I am passionate about sharing my work and connecting with fellow artists and art lovers.
