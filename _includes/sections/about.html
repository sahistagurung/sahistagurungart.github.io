About Me

I am an Artist whose work exists at the intersection of traditional Himalayan aesthetics and modern digital exploration. Based in Kathmandu, Nepal, I draw inspiration from the spiritual architecture and natural majesty of my surroundings to create emotive, textured pieces.

My portfolio, Sahista Gurung Art, represents a journey through various mediums—from detailed graphite studies of wildlife to vibrant, large-scale acrylic landscapes. I have a strong interest in AI and Web Development, viewing code as another medium for creative expression and problem-solving.

My goal is to continue exploring the connection between fine arts and technology, blending creativity with innovation. I am interested in collaborating on projects that combine thoughtful design with technical development.
